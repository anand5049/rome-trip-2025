Italy Family Trip: April 27 - May 3, 2025

Itinerary

Day 1: April 27 - Arrival in Venice
  • 10:00 AM: Arrive at Venice Marco Polo Airport
  • 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Transfer to Venice (Alilaguna Water Bus to San Marco, Family Event, €15/adult, €7.50/kids under 12, Book Here)
  • 3:00 PM: Check-in at Hotel Aqua Palace (Calle Pezzana, 380, Venice, Booking)
  • 3:30 PM - 6:00 PM: Explore St. Mark’s Square & Gelato (Piazza San Marco, Venice, Campanile Tickets: Book Here, Gelato at Gelateria Suso, Vegan Options)
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner at Rossopomodoro (Vegetarian Pizzas, Reservations: Website, Try Margherita)
  • Travel Tip: Arrive early for Alilaguna to avoid crowds. Bring water for kids during St. Mark’s visit.
  • Kid-Friendly: St. Mark’s pigeons are fun for kids to chase (supervised).
Day 2: April 28 - Venice Exploration
  • 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Doge’s Palace Secret Itineraries Tour (Doge’s Palace, Venice, Tickets: Book Here)
  • 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM: Shared Gondola Ride (Gondola Station near St. Mark’s Square, €80/30 mins, Split with Another Family)
  • 2:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Murano Glassblowing (Murano, Venice, Vaporetto Line 4.1, Free Demo of Venetian Glass Art)
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner at Trattoria da Remigio (Vegetarian Options, Try Risotto, Reservations: Website)
  • Travel Tip: Book Doge’s tour early—sells out fast. Vaporetto can be crowded; hold kids’ hands.
  • Kid-Friendly: Gondola ride is a hit—let kids spot bridges. Glassblowing demo fascinates with colors.
Day 3: April 29 - Venice to Florence
  • 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Train to Florence (Venezia Santa Lucia → Firenze Santa Maria Novella, Trenitalia, Tickets Booked)
  • 10:30 AM: Check-in at Hotel Spadai (Via de Martelli, Florence, Booking)
  • 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Florence Duomo & Lunch (Piazza del Duomo, Florence, Exterior Visit Only, Lunch at Mercato Centrale, Vegetarian Panini)
  • 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Uffizi Gallery (Uffizi Family Tour, Florence, Tickets: Book Here)
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner at Il Vegetariano (Vegetarian/Vegan, Via delle Ruote, Florence, Try Zucchini Lasagna)
  • Travel Tip: Train seats—book family section. Uffizi: Arrive 15 mins early for security.
  • Kid-Friendly: Duomo’s dome is a fun “giant hat” story for kids. Uffizi tour includes kid-friendly art explanations.
Day 4: April 30 - Day Trip to Pisa
  • 7:30 AM - 8:30 AM: Train to Pisa (Firenze Santa Maria Novella → Pisa Centrale, Trenitalia, €8/person)
  • 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Leaning Tower & Cathedral (Piazza del Duomo, Pisa, Free Cathedral Entry, Tower Tickets: Book Here)
  • 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM: Lunch at Il Crudo (Vegetarian Panini, Via San Francesco, 108, Pisa, Vegan Panini, Smoothies, Salads)
  • 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM: Train Back to Florence (Pisa Centrale → Firenze Santa Maria Novella)
  • 3:30 PM - 4:30 PM: Ponte Vecchio Walk (Family Event, Ponte Vecchio, Florence, Local Jewelry Shops, Street Performers)
  • 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM: Piazzale Michelangelo Picnic (Panoramic Views + Picnic, Florence, Grab Vegetarian Sandwiches/Snacks from Mercato Centrale)
  • 7:30 PM: Dinner at La Menagere (Vegetarian Options like Truffle Risotto, Via de’ Ginori, 8, Florence, Reservations: Website)
  • Travel Tip: Pisa Tower tickets—book timed entry. Bring sunscreen for Piazzale Michelangelo.
  • Kid-Friendly: Leaning Tower photo ops (pretend to “push” it). Ponte Vecchio performers entertain kids.
Day 5: May 1 - Florence to Rome
  • 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Train to Rome (Firenze Santa Maria Novella → Roma Termini, Trenitalia, Tickets Booked)
  • 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Check-in at Residenza Argentina (Via di Torre Argentina, 47, 00186 Roma RM, Booking)
  • 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM: Pantheon (Free Entry) & Lunch at Ristorante Roscioli (Vegetarian Pasta/Pizza, Try Cacio e Pepe, Via dei Giubbonari, 21, Call +39 06 687 5287)
  • 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Piazza Navona & Campo de’ Fiori (Kid-Friendly Stroll, Street Artists at Navona, Market Snacks at Campo)
  • 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM: Gelato at Giolitti (Vegan Sorbets, Via Uffici del Vicario, 40)
  • 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM: Dinner at Il Margutta (Gourmet Vegetarian, Try Artichoke Ravioli, Via Margutta, 118, Call +39 06 3265 0577)
  • Travel Tip: Train—arrive 15 mins early. Pantheon: Short visit, kids may tire quickly.
  • Kid-Friendly: Navona’s artists let kids watch; Campo’s fruit stalls are a fun snack stop.
Day 6: May 2 - Vatican City
  • 8:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel (Ticket PDF, GetYourGuide, Arrive 7:45 AM)
  • 10:45 AM - 11:45 AM: St. Peter’s Basilica (Free Entry, Piazza San Pietro)
  • 12:15 PM - 1:45 PM: Lunch at Pizzeria Amalfi (Vegan Pizza, Try Margherita, Via dei Gracchi, 12, Call +39 06 3973 3165)
  • 2:15 PM - 3:45 PM: Castel Sant’Angelo (Book Here, €15/adult, Kids Free)
  • 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM: Trevi Fountain (Piazza di Trevi, Toss Coins) & Gelato at Giolitti (Vegan Options)
  • 8:00 PM - 9:30 PM: Dinner at Mater Terrae (Vegetarian, Try Eggplant Parmigiana, Via della Dataria, 3, Call +39 06 679 3818)
  • Travel Tip: Vatican: Wear comfy shoes, long lines. Trevi: Evening is less crowded.
  • Kid-Friendly: Castel’s secret passage story excites kids. Trevi coin toss is a fun ritual.
Day 7: May 3 - Ancient Rome & Departure
  • 8:30 AM - 10:30 AM: Colosseum (Book Here, €20/adult, Kids Free + €2 Fee, Arrive 8:15 AM)
  • 10:45 AM - 12:15 PM: Roman Forum & Palatine Hill (Included in Colosseum Ticket)
  • 12:45 PM - 2:15 PM: Lunch at Ristorante Roscioli (Veggie Options, Try Gnocchi, Via dei Giubbonari, 21)
  • 2:45 PM - 5:00 PM: Villa Borghese Gardens (Rowboats €4/person, 00197 Roma RM)
  • 5:30 PM - 6:15 PM: Taxi to Hotel La Magnolia 140 (Fiumicino, €50-60)
  • 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM: Dinner at Hotel or Ristorante Rustichella (Veggie Pasta, Try Pesto, Via Giuseppe Giulietti, 3, Call +39 06 650 7235)
  • Travel Tip: Colosseum: Bring water, sunny. Villa Borghese: Relaxed end—kids can run.
  • Kid-Friendly: Colosseum gladiator stories engage kids. Villa Borghese rowboats are a fun family activity.
